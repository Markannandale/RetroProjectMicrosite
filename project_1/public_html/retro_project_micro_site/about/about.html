Hoover cleaning products are designed to fit the unique cleaning needs of their users. Users can choose from a variety of different vacuums, including upright, canister, stick, and hand-vacs with HEPA bags for pet owners and users with allergies. There are also bagless vacuums with powerful cyclonic filtration for quick and easy dirt disposal.
