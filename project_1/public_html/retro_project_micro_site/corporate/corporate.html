The Hoover Company was founded in 1908 by Murray Spangler and W.H. Hoover.As a leader of the floor care industry, it manufactures and supplies vacuum cleaners for general and special-purpose vacuuming for homes and commercial settings.Today, Hoover is part of TTI Floor Care North America. Hoover has always been trusted by consumers and businesses to provide reliable, innovative cleaning solutions.
